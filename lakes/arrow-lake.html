Arrow Lakes Fishing Charters

Upper and Lower Arrow are two of the most dramatic fisheries in southern BC — deep, clear, hemmed in by mountains, and home to the legendary Gerrard.

Book an Arrow Charter 250-902-8323
The fishery

Peaceful, scenic, big-fish capable

The Arrow Lakes system — Upper and Lower Arrow, connected by the Columbia River — draws anglers from around North America for its Gerrard a landlocked strain famous for growing to 20+ pounds. In addition to Gerrards, Arrow holds, kokanee salmon, with fantastic fall fishing in cooling water.

Species
, kokanee

Spring (Gerrard spawn run) and Fall
Technique

Arrow trips are our most scenic — vertical mountain walls, glacier-fed inflows, long still stretches of pristine water. Come for the fish, stay for the view.
