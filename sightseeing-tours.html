What's on offer

Private, captained, paced for you

Our sightseeing tours run the same 26-foot Kingfisher 2525 as our fishing charters — so you get a heated cabin, a proper head, a fridge for your drinks, and plenty of deck space for photos. Tours are fully custom: tell us what you want to see and how long you want to be out, and we'll build the route.

Popular options include Okanagan Lake sunset cruises past the cliffs of Bear Creek, hidden-cove exploration on Shuswap Lake, and full-day scenic runs on the Arrow Lakes through some of the most dramatic mountain scenery in southern BC.
