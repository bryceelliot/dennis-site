What to expect

A proper Okanagan fishing day

Every Kelowna fishing charter with Outdoor Adventures BC is private — your group, your captain, your pace. We meet you at a Kelowna-area launch, load up, and head out to the water the fish are holding on that day. Expect downrigger trolling for kokanee and rainbow, jigging for lake trout in the deep, and a working knowledge of the Okanagan that comes from fishing it year-round.

Rods, reels, tackle, bait, downriggers and life jackets are all onboard. Bring your BC fishing licence, polarized sunglasses, a hat, layers (it's cool on the water even in July), snacks, and whatever you'd like to drink. Lunch is available on request.
