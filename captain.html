About the captain

Why owner-captained matters

When you book with Outdoor Adventures BC, the person who answers the phone is the same person who rigs your rod, backs the trailer down the ramp, and reads the fish-finder on your trip. That's Dennis — owner-captain, not a contractor, not a different guy each day.

Dennis fishes Okanagan Lake year-round. Kokanee in June, rainbow trout through the cool months, rainbow trout on the shoulder seasons, and the occasional dawn run up to Shuswap or Arrow when the bite dictates. He knows which point holds fish this week because he was there last week.

The goal on every charter is simple: show you a version of the Okanagan you can't see from shore, and put you on fish. Newcomer, experienced angler, family of five — Dennis tunes the day to you.
